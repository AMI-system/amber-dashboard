Welcome to AMBER

Automated Monitoring of Biodiversity with Edge Processing and Remote Sensing: A cutting-edge collaboration between UKCEH and The Alan Turing Institute, exploring wildlife conservation through AI-powered camera trap networks.

The AMBER project represents a novel approach to biodiversity monitoring, combining advanced camera trap technology with machine learning algorithms to automatically identify and track wildlife populations across diverse ecosystems.

Our intelligent monitoring systems are deployed worldwide, from the rainforests of Costa Rica to the deserts of Namibia, providing unprecedented insights into ecosystem health and wildlife behavior patterns.
