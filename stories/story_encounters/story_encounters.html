Wildlife Encounters

Our AMBER camera traps capture, more than just moths, also the incredible diversity of wildlife across different ecosystems. Discover the fascinating creatures that trigger our AI-powered monitoring systems.

Beyond moths, our cameras reveal the complete ecosystem story. Every frog strike, gecko interaction, and curious cat investigation captured contributes to our understanding of wildlife behavior, habitat preferences, and ecosystem health. These unexpected non-moth encounters provide crucial insights into biodiversity patterns and ecological relationships that directly support conservation efforts and policy decisions.
