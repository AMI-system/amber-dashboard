Global Remote Biodiversity Monitoring

Our global network provides insights into wildlife behavior and population dynamics across diverse ecosystems. From tropical rainforests to arid rangelands, AMBER's AI-powered camera traps are revolutionising how we understand and protect biodiversity.

Collaborative Research

Each deployment site represents a unique collaboration between local institutions, researchers, and conservation organisations. This partnership model ensures that our technology serves local conservation needs while contributing to global scientific understanding.
